Energy Transition Tracker Work in progress
Progress on the energy transition can be measured on two major dimensions: electrification of the total energy supply, and decarbonization of the electricity grid (transition to renewable and nuclear power generation). Taken together, these dimensions give us a rough picture of how the transition is progressing around the world.
