Energy Transition Tracker Work in progress
Progress on the energy transition can be measured on two major dimensions: electrification of the total energy supply, and transition of the electricity grid to low-carbon fuels (renewables and nuclear). Taken together, these dimensions give us a rough picture of how the transition is progressing around the world.
